# Great Blue Heron Research, Monitoring, and Learning Resources

A curated directory of authoritative sources, live cameras, monitoring programs, and organizations for anyone building content or programs about the Great Blue Heron.

## Core species accounts (start here)

| Resource | What it offers |
|---|---|
| [Cornell Lab — All About Birds](https://www.allaboutbirds.org/guide/Great_Blue_Heron/) | The standard public species account: ID, life history, sounds, maps |
| [Birds of the World — Great Blue Heron](https://birdsoftheworld.org/bow/species/grbher3/cur/introduction) | The definitive scholarly account (Vennesland & Butler 2020); subscription |
| [Audubon Field Guide](https://www.audubon.org/field-guide/bird/great-blue-heron) | Accessible life-history text plus climate-vulnerability data |
| [eBird species page](https://ebird.org/species/grbher3) | Live distribution maps, abundance, recent sightings near you |
| [American Bird Conservancy](https://abcbirds.org/birds/great-blue-heron/) | Conservation-framed life history |
| [HeronConservation (IUCN SSC Heron Specialist Group)](https://heronconservation.org/) | Global heron science, Red List assessments, journal |

## Government and agency sources

- [USGS Bird Banding Laboratory — Longevity Records](https://www.usgs.gov/labs/bird-banding-laboratory) — source of the 23–25 year lifespan record.
- [Maine IF&W SGCN report](https://www.maine.gov/ifw/wildlife/reports/pdfs/SGCN_Reports/SGCN/Great%20Blue%20Heron__Ardea%20herodias.pdf) — model for state-level status writing.
- [Government of Canada — fannini Management Plan](https://www.canada.ca/en/environment-climate-change/services/species-risk-public-registry/management-plans/great-blue-heron-fannini-2016-proposed.html) — Pacific subspecies conservation.
- [BC Conservation Status Reports](https://a100.gov.bc.ca/pub/eswp/esr.do?id=17626) — interior population detail.
- [California Wildlife Habitat Relationships (CDFW)](https://nrm.dfg.ca.gov/FileHandler.ashx?DocumentID=1571) — habitat requirements.
- [NC Wildlife Resources Commission profile](https://www.ncwildlife.gov/media/1885/open) — colony survey data and buffer guidance.
- [State of the St. Lawrence monitoring](https://www.planstlaurent.qc.ca/en/state-monitoring/monitoring-sheets/great-blue-heron-2015) — contaminant sentinel program.
- [Oregon Forests — GBH and Forestry](https://oregonforests.org/sites/default/files/2018-04/WIMF_data_Great_Blue_Heron.pdf) and [BC Wildlife Habitat Features Field Guide](https://www2.gov.bc.ca/assets/gov/environment/natural-resource-policy-legislation/legislation-regulation/frpa-pac/wildlife-habitat-features/whf_field_guide_kootenay_boundary_grblueheron.pdf) — land-management rules.
- [NatureServe Explorer](https://explorer.natureserve.org/Taxon/ELEMENT_GLOBAL.2.100203/Ardea_herodias) — conservation ranks.

## Live cams and media

- **Cornell Lab Bird Cams — Sapsucker Woods Great Blue Herons**: streaming since 2012 from a nest used since 2009; archives include egg-laying, owl defense, and fledging highlights.[^1^]
- [Macaulay Library](https://www.macaulaylibrary.org/) — photos, video, and the definitive audio collection (search "Great Blue Heron").
- [Xeno-canto](https://xeno-canto.org/) — community sound archive for calls.

## Community science and monitoring programs

| Program | Role |
|---|---|
| eBird | Report sightings; powers range and trend science |
| North American Breeding Bird Survey (USGS) | Source of the 1966–2019 increasing trend |
| Christmas Bird Count (Audubon) | Century-long winter dataset |
| Audubon Florida Colony Watch / Venice Rookery Watch | Colony data since 1997, shared with Florida FWC[^2^] |
| Maine Heron Observation Network | Volunteer colony monitoring + GPS-tagging ("Harper")[^3^] |
| Stanley Park Ecology Society — Adopt a Heron Nest | Urban colony stewardship in Vancouver[^4^] |
| Heron Habitat Helpers (Seattle) | Habitat restoration and policy for the official city bird[^5^] |

## Key scientific literature (for citation-grade content)

- Vennesland, R. G. & R. W. Butler (2020). Great Blue Heron (*Ardea herodias*), version 1.0. *Birds of the World*. — the master reference.
- Butler, R. W. (1997). *The Great Blue Heron: A Natural History and Ecology of a Seashore Sentinel*. UBC Press.
- McGuire, H. L. et al. (2019). Evaluating the taxonomic status of the Great White Heron. *The Auk* 136. — genetics behind the species debate.[^6^]
- Owen, D. F. Mortality of the Great Blue Heron as shown by banding. *The Auk* — 71% first-year mortality figure.[^7^]
- Champoux, L. et al. (2006). Contamination and biomarkers in the great blue heron, an indicator of the state of the St. Lawrence River. *Ecotoxicology* 15: 83–96.[^8^]
- Dickerman, R. W. (2004). A review of the subspecies of the Great Blue Heron. *Proc. Biol. Soc. Washington* 117: 242–250.[^6^]
